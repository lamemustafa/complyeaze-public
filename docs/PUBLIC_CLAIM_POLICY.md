# Public Claim Policy

Public pages must be true, source-backed, and narrow.

## Evidence Requirements

Any claim about security, privacy, release status, store status, browser-local
behavior, statutory coverage, professional review, integrations, production
readiness, route ownership transfer, route cleanup readiness, redirect
readiness, or rollback readiness must point to one of:

- Local source code in this repository.
- A public release, changelog, or status artifact.
- A reviewed public facts snapshot.
- A linked official or primary source.
- A synthetic runtime screenshot or test artifact.

## Banned Without Separate Review

Do not claim:

- Government approval.
- GSTN, CBIC, or other official portal-operator endorsement.
- ComplyEaze, Axal, Pack, Tools, or Sanchika trademark rights beyond
  [LICENSE.brand.md](../LICENSE.brand.md) and [TRADEMARKS.md](../TRADEMARKS.md).
- ComplyEaze endorsement, ownership, operation, partnership, or affiliation for
  forks or downstream deployments unless the maintainers explicitly operate
  that surface.
- DPDP compliance.
- Certified GST filing behavior.
- Audit-proof output.
- Zero data collection.
- Nothing leaves your device.
- CA verified.
- Production readiness for a surface that is still alpha, source-build, or
  externally blocked.

## Sensitive Data

Use synthetic data only. Never use PAN, GSTIN, Aadhaar, phone numbers, taxpayer
names, portal HTML, notices, credentials, documents, OTPs, CAPTCHA images,
cookies, tokens, local paths, or production screenshots as public evidence.

## Review

If a claim cannot be proved, narrow the language until it matches evidence or
remove it.
