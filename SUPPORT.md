# Support

Use public issues for public-site bugs, documentation gaps, broken links,
accessibility issues, and synthetic-data examples.

Do not post:

- PAN, GSTIN, Aadhaar, phone numbers, taxpayer names, or client names.
- GST portal HTML, notices, replies, orders, documents, PDFs, or screenshots
  containing real data.
- Credentials, OTPs, CAPTCHA screenshots, cookies, tokens, browser profiles, or
  local filesystem paths.
- Private deployment, billing, or infrastructure details.

For vulnerabilities or sensitive-data exposure, follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md)
instead of opening a public issue.
