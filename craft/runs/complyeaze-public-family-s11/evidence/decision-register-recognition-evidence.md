# Decision register recognition evidence

**Evidence label:** `ai-recognition-proxy`; this is not user validation.

Three isolated matchers received the semantic-blind and identity-blind boards
alongside the three candidate territory descriptions. Each matched both boards
to **Decision register** without relying on readable product/brand text or
accent colour.

| Proxy | Correct matchers | Non-colour basis |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| `decision-register-semantic-blind.webp` | 3/3 | Full-width bordered ledger, three-column header, repeated horizontal records, narrow identity field, central fact stack, fixed continuation field. |
| `decision-register-identity-blind.webp` | 3/3 | The same register topology, row rhythm, internal fact matrix, short left-cell rules, and fixed action alignment. |

The evidence excludes Evidence rail (persistent asymmetric side rail) and
Custody cascade (progressive single-column offset sequence).
