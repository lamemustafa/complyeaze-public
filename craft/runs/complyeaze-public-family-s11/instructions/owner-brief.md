# S11 owner brief — ComplyEaze public product-family section

## Owner direction

The owner confirmed the whole public homepage as the surface and limited S11 to
the product-family section. Preserve the existing header, hero, trust action,
typed public-route content, and evidence-register identity. Do not touch the
private ComplyEaze application.

## User outcome

A prospective buyer should distinguish Axal, Pack, and Tools by job, custody
boundary, public proof, current posture, and safe next action before leaving
the ComplyEaze public site.

## Constraints

- Use the current canonical public route manifest; do not invent product,
  release, privacy, security, or readiness claims.
- Retain the ComplyEaze deep-teal evidence-register register; product signals
  orient but never become competing consumer identities.
- Static Astro only: zero authored client JavaScript, no remote assets, and no
  generated raster dependency.
- The section must remain readable and equivalent at 390px and 1440px,
  keyboard navigable, reduced-motion safe, forced-colors legible, and free of
  horizontal overflow.
- This is direction approval work, not production approval. A separate owner
  production decision remains required after implementation and verification.

## Explicit non-goals

- Hero, site navigation, footer, trust route, product gateways, Axal public
  application, Pack source/release repository, or Tools product repository.
- Forms, accounts, uploads, analytics, runtime integrations, private data, or
  unverified claims.
