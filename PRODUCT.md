# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

ComplyEaze Public serves CA firms, CS teams, compliance operators, business
owners, prospective design partners, open-source reviewers, and contributors who
need to understand the ComplyEaze product family without accessing private app
data.

## Purpose

The public site explains what ComplyEaze, Axal, Pack, Tools, and Sanchika do,
what each surface owns, what is public, what is private, what evidence supports
claims, and how users can evaluate or contribute safely.

## Voice

Professional, precise, evidence-backed, and legally cautious. Public copy should
be plain enough for business readers and exact enough for technical reviewers.

## Non-Goals

- Do not behave like the authenticated Axal app.
- Do not collect sensitive taxpayer data.
- Do not imply statutory advice, government approval, or production readiness
  beyond verified evidence.
