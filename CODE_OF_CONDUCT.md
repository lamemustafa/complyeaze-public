# Code Of Conduct

Be professional, constructive, and careful with sensitive compliance context.

## Expected Behavior

- Discuss issues and reviews with evidence.
- Keep criticism specific to code, design, content, process, or risk.
- Use synthetic data in examples.
- Redact taxpayer identifiers, portal data, credentials, documents, local paths,
  and screenshots before sharing.
- Respect maintainer decisions on security disclosure timing and trademark use.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or discriminatory comments.
- Publishing private vulnerability details before maintainers can triage.
- Sharing credentials, real taxpayer data, private documents, or customer
  information in issues, PRs, commits, screenshots, or chat.
- Misrepresenting affiliation, endorsement, statutory correctness, or government
  approval.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove content that violates this policy or
exposes sensitive data. Maintainers may also restrict repository access for
repeated or severe violations.
